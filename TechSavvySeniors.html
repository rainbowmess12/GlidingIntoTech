Welcome to Tech Savvy Seniors

Tech Savvy Seniors is a workshop dedicated to helping older adults navigate the world of technology with ease. Our goal is to make technology simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. We cover topics like using smartphones, understanding basic software, online security, and more.

Our sessions are designed to be hands-on and interactive, allowing participants to ask questions and learn at their own pace. Whether you want to learn how to video chat with loved ones, browse the internet safely, or explore apps, we’re here to guide you every step of the way!

Join us at Tech Savvy Seniors to discover how technology can make your life easier and more connected. We’re excited to help you become more confident and comfortable in the digital world.
