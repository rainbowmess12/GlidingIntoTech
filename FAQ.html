Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Gliding Into Tech?

Gliding Into Tech is an organization aimed at helping individuals of all ages to enhance their technology skills. We offer interactive workshops, including Starting From Scratch for children and Tech Savvy Seniors for older adults.

2. Where are the workshops located?

Our workshops are based in New Jersey. We currently offer in-person sessions at various locations and are also exploring virtual options for wider accessibility.

3. What is the Starting From Scratch workshop?

Starting From Scratch is a hands-on workshop where children learn the basics of coding using the Scratch platform. Participants will create their own projects while developing essential programming skills.

4. What does the Tech Savvy Seniors workshop cover?

Tech Savvy Seniors is designed to help older adults become more comfortable with technology. Topics include using smartphones, understanding AI, navigating common software applications, and maintaining cybersecurity.

5. Do you offer virtual workshops?

Yes! We are in the process of offering virtual workshops through platforms like Zoom to reach more participants. To reach out for a possible Zoom workshop, check out the "Contact" page located in the navigation bar.

6. Can I suggest a topic for a future workshop?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our community. Please reach out to us through the "Contact" page with your ideas.

7. How can I contact you for further questions?

For any additional inquiries, please visit our "Contact" page, where you can find our email address and contact form.

8. Are the workshops free?

Yes. We strive to keep our sessions affordable and accessible.

9. Who can attend the workshops?

Our workshops cater to a diverse audience. Starting From Scratch is designed for children, while Tech Savvy Seniors targets older adults. However, anyone interested in learning about technology is welcome!
