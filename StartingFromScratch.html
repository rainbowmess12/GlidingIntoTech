Starting From Scratch - Empowering Kids with Coding

Starting From Scratch is a workshop designed to teach children the fundamentals of block coding through fun and interactive projects. As part of Gliding Into Tech, I lead these workshops, helping kids develop a love for coding while building creative projects on the Scratch platform.

Scratch allows kids to create animations, games, and stories, all while learning core computational thinking concepts. In this workshop, kids are encouraged to explore their creativity, collaborate with peers, and gain confidence in their technical skills.

By nurturing young minds and giving them a solid foundation in coding, we are preparing the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers. With each project, we are opening doors for children to explore technology in new and exciting ways.
